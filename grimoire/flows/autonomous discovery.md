The following describes the autonomous discovery flow.


* [[sigil]] - discovers potential vulnerabilities 
* [[familiar]] - triages the discoveries 
	* [[librarian]] - often aids in initial triage
	* [[proof of concept]] - the familiar commissions a proof of concept for the finding
		* [[gnome]] - implements the proof of concept
	* [[finding]] - the familiar constructs a finding
		* [[librarian]] - potential references
